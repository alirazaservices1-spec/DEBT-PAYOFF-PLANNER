Redesign Dex character and all 8 animation 
states to match the official design brief.

CHARACTER DESIGN:
- Compact rounded fox/woodland creature
- NOT a bird, NOT green, NOT like Duolingo owl
- Head = 45% of total height
- Eyes = 35% of face width
- Short limbs, round body
- Primary color: warm orange-amber #E8850A
- Cream/white belly and inner ear
- Bright expressive eyes, large pupils
- Bold outlines, 2D vector style
- Must be readable at 40x40px

8 ANIMATION STATES using React Native Animated:

1. IDLE (default, always running):
- Gentle vertical bob
- Body rises 6px and returns
- 3.0s loop
- Eyes slow blink every 4-5s
- Tail sways slightly out of phase

2. HAPPY (fires on every payment logged):
- Body compresses then launches 12px up
- Arms raise to 60 degrees
- Eyes widen to 120% size
- Duration 0.5s
- Returns to idle automatically

3. CELEBRATING (level up, milestone, debt cleared):
- Jump + 180 degree spin
- Arms fully raised
- Eyes sparkle (4-point star overlay)
- Duration 0.8-1.2s
- Loops 2-3x for big milestones
- 360 spin for legendary moments

4. WORRIED (streak at risk):
- Head tilts left 15 degrees
- Arms forward in cupping motion
- Eyes narrow to concerned shape
- Left-right head shake 0.3s period
- Continuous loop

5. SLEEPING (7+ days no app open):
- Eyes closed (curved lines)
- Zzz floats up, 1.5s cycle
- Minimal breathing movement
- Overall opacity 70%

6. ENCOURAGING (3+ days no payment):
- Thumbs up pose
- Head nod 1.0s
- Warm half-squint eyes
- Speech bubble: "You've got this. 
  No judgment here."

7. ONBOARDING CLIPBOARD:
- Holds clipboard in both arms
- Tail raised (alert/excited)
- Finger taps clipboard in sync 
  with speech bubble typing

8. SURPRISED (variable bonus XP):
- Eyes wide to 150%
- Mouth open O shape
- Small jump, arms spread
- 0.4s duration
- Transitions to celebrating

DEX SPEECH BUBBLE:
- Rounded rect, 16px corner radius
- White fill
- 1px border #E8600A
- Tail from bottom-left toward Dex mouth
- Text types character by character 40ms each
- Cursor blinks while typing
- Bubble entrance: scale 0.8 to 1.0 
  over 0.2s with spring easing
- Max 2 lines of text

DEX IS TAPPABLE:
- Tapping Dex triggers random encouraging 
  speech bubble from a list of 8 messages
- Small bounce animation on tap
