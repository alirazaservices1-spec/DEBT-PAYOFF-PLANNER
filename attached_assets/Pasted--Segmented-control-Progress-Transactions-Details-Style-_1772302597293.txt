   with large celebration text that scales in

## CONSTRAINTS

- Pure SwiftUI only — no UIKit except for haptics and biometrics

- No third-party animation libraries — SwiftUI animations only
- All charts interactive (tap for tooltips)
- Dark mode IS the design (no light mode needed)

- No force unwraps
- Every screen works with empty state (0 debts) — show illustrated empty states
- Empty states: dark background, subtle illustration built in SwiftUI 
  (NOT a flat gray ghost image like the screenshots show)
