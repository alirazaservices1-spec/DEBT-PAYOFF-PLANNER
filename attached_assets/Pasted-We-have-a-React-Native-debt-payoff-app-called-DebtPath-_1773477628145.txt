In the recommendation engine:

## 3. FIX SNOWBALL VS AVALANCHE FORMULAS

The Snowball and Avalanche strategies are currently showing the same 
total interest saved, which is incorrect.

Please fix the payoff calculation engine:
- Snowball: pay minimum on all debts, put all extra payment toward 
  the SMALLEST BALANCE first until gone, then roll that payment to 
  the next smallest
- Avalanche: pay minimum on all debts, put all extra payment toward 
  the HIGHEST APR first until gone, then roll that payment to the 
  next highest APR
- Each month: calculate interest FIRST on remaining balance, then 
  apply payment
- Total interest paid must be calculated separately for each strategy
- Avalanche should show less total interest than Snowball when there 
  are debts with meaningfully different APRs
- Test with at least these sample debts before finishing:
  - Chase: $26,000 at 25% APR, $200/month minimum
  - Personal: $5,000 at 15% APR, $150/month minimum
  - IRS: $25,000 at 0% (no payment plan)
- Log results to console so they can be verified

---

## 4. IRS DEBT — DAILY COMPOUNDING INTEREST

For any debt categorized as IRS/tax debt:
- Interest should compound DAILY not monthly
- Daily rate = APR / 365
- Each day: balance = balance * (1 + daily rate)
- At end of month apply the monthly payment
- Make sure this is handled separately from standard monthly 
  compounding debts in the payoff engine
