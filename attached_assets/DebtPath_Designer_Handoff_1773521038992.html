01 — Core Color Palette

Color System

Four emotional signals drive every color decision: warmth (orange), trust (blue), growth (green), achievement (gold). Every color must reinforce one of these exactly.

PRIMARY
Dex Orange
#E8600A
Streak flame, mascot, celebrations, urgency. The emotional heartbeat of the app.

XP bars, levels, primary action buttons. Signals stability. Never use for warnings.

Interest saved, extra payment wins, debt-free date. Reserved for good news only.

#D4A017
Badges, trophies, streak shields, level-up. Aspirational. Use sparingly.

⚠️ Streak at risk ONLY. Never use for debt balance display. Max 2 screens total.

🚨 Critical rule: Debt balance is NEVER shown in red. Red is reserved exclusively for streak emergencies. Seeing a red number next to their debt total frames the user as a failure rather than someone solving a problem.

06 — Sound Design

Audio Specifications

Every sound must be something users look forward to. Never punitive. If a sound tests as "stressful" in user research, redesign it. The test: would you want to hear this every day?

💰
Payment logged
Single warm coin-drop tone. Pitch ~C5 (~523Hz). Marimba or vibraphone timbre. Soft attack (10ms), resonant body, 300ms decay. Stereo centered. 0.4s total.
Reference: Duolingo correct-answer / Apple Pay confirm — but warmer. NOT a cash register. NOT silence.
🔥
Streak maintained
Two-note ascending chord. C5→E5, major second interval. 0.3s soft attack, 0.4s total. Warm resonance tail. Marimba timbre preferred.
Reference: Duolingo streak continuation chime. Warm xylophone feel.
📈
XP earned
Short high bell. Pitch ~E5 (~659Hz). 0.15s attack, 0.15s decay, 0.3s total. Brighter and shorter than payment sound. Must be distinguishable from payment sound by pitch and duration.
Reference: Pokémon item pickup / light notification chime.
⬆️
Level up
3-note ascending fanfare. C5→E5→G5 (major triad). 0.25s per note, 0.15s overlap. Full 0.8s. Warm brass or synth timbre. Satisfying resolution on G5.
Reference: Duolingo league promotion / shortened Zelda item fanfare.
⚠️
Streak at risk (notification)
Two-note DESCENDING minor second. E5→D#5. Soft attack. Low urgency — concerned, NOT alarming. 0.3s total. Must feel like a friend tapping your shoulder, not an alarm.
Reference: iMessage notification but lower and softer. If it feels stressful, make it softer.
✨
Variable bonus day (surprise)
Upward sparkle: 3 rapid ascending notes E5→G5→C6 at 80ms apart. Bright, surprising, delightful. 0.35s total. Like finding a hidden coin.
Coin sparkle / discovery sound. Fires randomly 1-2x per month — must feel genuinely surprising each time.
💚
Interest saved (weekly)
Three overlapping plinks: E5, G5, C6 at 100ms offsets. Each 0.2s duration. Feels like coins landing on felt — not harsh, not hollow. DebtPath signature sound. 0.5s total.
Original DebtPath signature — no direct Duolingo parallel. Invest in making this distinctive and warm.
🎉
Debt paid off — The Grand Finale
Full 3.0s celebration: ascending fanfare C5→E5→G5→C6 (0.8s) + confetti crackle (soft broadband noise, 0.5s, very low volume) + triumphant major chord C+E+G+C8va (1.0s held) + resonant decay (0.7s). Full stereo mix. PRODUCTION BUDGET ITEM.
Game show winner / fireworks finale. Users will remember this moment for years. It justifies the entire product. Hire a composer for this one.
